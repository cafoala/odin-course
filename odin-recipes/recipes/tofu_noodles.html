Tofu Noodles Recipe

Description

This tofu noodles recipe is a quick and easy dish that combines tofu, noodles, and fresh vegetables in a flavorful sauce. It's perfect for a healthy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 1 block (400g) firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown on all sides. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add sesame oil and saut√© the minced garlic and grated ginger for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the bell peppers, broccoli, and carrot to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the cooked tofu to the skillet. Add the cooked rice noodles and soy sauce. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with chopped green onions or sesame seeds if desired. Enjoy!
