Mushroom Pie Recipe

Description

This mushroom pie is a savory dish filled with a variety of mushrooms, herbs, and cheese, all encased in a flaky pie crust. It's perfect for a comforting meal any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 cups mixed mushrooms (such as cremini, shiitake, and button), sliced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (such as Gruyère or cheddar)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they release their moisture and become tender, about 8-10 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh thyme leaves.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and let the mushroom mixture cool slightly. Stir in the shredded cheese and heavy cream until well combined.
  6. Roll out the pie crust and fit it into a 9-inch pie dish. Trim any excess dough from the edges.
  7. Pour the mushroom filling into the pie crust, spreading it out evenly.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is set.
  9. Let the pie cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy
